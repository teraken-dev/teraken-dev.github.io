Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-30

doneday respects your privacy.

Overview

doneday is a habit tracking app. The app lets you create habits, mark them as completed, and review streaks and recent progress.

Data Stored In The App

Habit names, icons, colors, completion logs, and streak data are stored locally on your device so the app can function.

Purchases

If you choose to remove ads with the app's one-time purchase, purchase processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play, and purchase status is managed with RevenueCat.

Advertising

The app may show ads through Google Mobile Ads. Ad-related SDKs may collect device identifiers, diagnostics, and app interaction data as described by their platform policies.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat
  • Google Mobile Ads

Tracking

The app does not currently implement an in-app tracking authorization flow.

Notifications

The current version of the app does not actively provide user-facing notification features.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age required by your local laws.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact terai.kenzi@gmail.com.
